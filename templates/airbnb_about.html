Problem

Airbnb has implemented a new program called Airbnb Plus. Airbnb mark listings that they consider to be"Beautiful homes. Exceptional hosts. Verified for quality.". These listings areverified inperson before being approved to qualify. Also, as a host you would need to have a overall rating of 4.8 andhaven't cancelled a reservation in the past year. They do have a checklist avaliable on their website, however they seem to be more like guidelines rather than requirements.
Why would owners want to go through the effort to achieve this rating? According to Airbnb:
  • Plus badge
  • Elevated placement and better visibility in search results
  • All-new design and layout for listing page
  • Professional photography
  • 7 times more listing views
  • 70% more nights book
